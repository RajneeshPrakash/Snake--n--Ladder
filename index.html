Guidelines to Play
  • Player
    • 1-Player

      • In this mode, player plays with computer. Every next turn will be automatically played by computer.

    • 2-Player

      • In this mode, player plays with another player. Every next turn will be played by each of them.

    • 3-Player ( Currenlty not Supported )

      • In this mode, 3 player play this game at a time. Every next turn will be played by each of them in order. Game will automatically guide them about whose turn it is?.

  • Game Rule
    • Mode

      • A player has to choose the game mode and register his name and also choose the coin colour.

    • Win

      • Each player can click on the dice cup in their respective order. Based on the dice point received by them, their respective coin will be moved accordingly automatically. To start the game a player must get six to move his coin for the first time. When a player gets six, he gets another chance to throw the dice to accumulate more points. Player who reaches to 100th place with the fastest move will be decided as winner.

Game Description

Snakes and Ladders is an ancient Indian board game regarded today as a worldwide classic. It is played between two or more players on a gameboard having numbered, gridded squares. A number of "ladders" and "snakes" are pictured on the board, each connecting two specific board squares. The object of the game is to navigate one's game piece, according to die rolls, from the start (bottom square) to the finish (top square), helped or hindered by ladders and snakes respectively.
